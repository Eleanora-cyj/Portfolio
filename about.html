My leisure time

I have a wide range of hobbies. I have learned Go, an abstract strategy board game, since I was young. It has trained my calculation ability and the big picture concept. Black and white, advance and retreat, the world of Go requires discipline, patience, judgment and strategy. Go can also teach you to let go of your obsession with winning or losing, and to think about problems from different angles.

As an important component of Chinese culture, Chinese calligraphy is a fascinating art, combining visual creativity and interpretation of the literary meaning. Sometimes when I am alone, I write calligraphy. It calms me down and concentrates on doing one thing. Its charm lies not in how cool the characters are, how fast you write, or how many characters you finish, but in the process of writing calligraphy, you will talk with your heart and have some inspirations. In the process of learning calligraphy, I also learned more about the poems and articles of various ancient dynasties. One of my favorite verses is “山重水复疑无路，柳暗花明又一村”, which means: where the hills and streams end and there seems no road beyond, amidst shading willows and blooming flowers another village appears.

I like listening to songs, the world of music makes me intoxicated. I used to be a member of my high school chorus, where I dared to sing for the first time and also learned a lot of singing skills. We went to many stages to perform together and won many awards.

I like traveling with my family and friends. Plan a rough schedule and leave the remaining details to luck and opportunity. I enjoy the scenery, food and people from all over the world that I met during the journey, and use the camera to record all the good memories.
