My Activities

My university life is wonderful, colored by internship, extracurriculum activities, volunteering and my own hobbies.

I began to practice calligraphy when I was very young. It exercised my patience and carefulness. The following are two of my recent works. They are Chinese poems. The left one means "asked it to be so clear, for the source of running water", and the right one means "life is a master, and death is also a ghost."

Teaching Assistant

In Fall 2019, I taught Physics lab -- Solar Cells: I-V Characteristics for 318 students in 21 labs. I introduced the background and related knowledge, did demonstrations, designed quiz questions and graded the reports.

Office Assistant

I was an assistant of the International Program Office. I was responsible for the photography exhibition, hosted the first "Go Global Day", publicized the buddy program and recruited volunteers of buddies for exchange students. I also edited guidebook for exchange programs and planned orientation, led Shanghai tours and organized cultural activities such as Tai Chi classes for international students.
